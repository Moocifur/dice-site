About the Craft

Welcome to Crystal Dice Crafts! I'm Sarah, and I've been creating handmade dice for tabletop gaming enthusiasts for over two years. What started as a hobby for my own D&D campaigns has grown into a passion for creating unique, high-quality dice that bring magic to every roll.

Each die is carefully crafted using premium resin, hand-mixed pigments, and special inclusions like glitter, dried flowers, or metallic foils. I take pride in ensuring every die is properly balanced and the numbers are crisp and easy to read.

Whether you're a seasoned dungeon master, a Magic: The Gathering player, or someone who just loves beautiful dice, I create pieces that are both functional and works of art. Every set tells a story and brings a little extra magic to your gaming table.
